The Implementation Process

We follow a structured approach to ensure AI adoption sticks.

Discovery (Week 1-2)

We audit your current finance stack, workflows, and pain points. We identify where AI can deliver the highest ROI—whether that's forecasting, reconciliation, reporting, or AP/AR automation.

Design (Week 3)

We recommend specific tools and design the integration architecture. No generic advice—we build a concrete implementation roadmap tailored to your systems, team, and budget.

Implementation (Weeks 4-8)

We configure and deploy the AI tools, integrate them with your existing accounting and ERP systems, and build the workflows that connect everything together.

Optimization (Ongoing)

We train your team, monitor adoption, and fine-tune the tools based on real-world performance. The goal: your finance team runs faster and smarter, permanently.
