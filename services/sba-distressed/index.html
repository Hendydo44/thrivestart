SBA Distressed Situations

When an SBA loan goes sideways, you need someone who knows the playbook. We help you navigate defaults, workouts, and recovery.

Navigate SBA Defaults with Confidence

SBA loan defaults create a unique set of pressures. Between the lender, the SBA, and potentially the U.S. Treasury, borrowers and guarantors face a complex web of obligations, timelines, and negotiation windows.

We've helped businesses and guarantors work through these situations by understanding the SBA Standard Operating Procedures, building credible workout proposals, and negotiating resolutions that preserve as much value as possible.

The SBA Workout Process

Every SBA situation is different, but the path forward follows a proven structure.

Assessment (Week 1)

We review the loan documents, SBA authorization, guaranty agreements, and current default status. We map the timeline and identify which stage of the SBA process you're in.

Strategy (Week 2)

Based on your financial position and the SBA's Standard Operating Procedures, we build a workout strategy—whether that's an offer-in-compromise, structured repayment, or litigation defense.

Negotiation (Weeks 3-8)

We prepare and submit formal proposals to the lender or SBA, manage back-and-forth communications, and negotiate terms that reflect your actual ability to pay.

Resolution (Ongoing)

Once terms are agreed, we help you execute the settlement, manage compliance requirements, and ensure the matter is fully closed with all parties.
