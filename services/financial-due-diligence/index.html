What We Deliver

A clear-eyed view of the target's financial reality—not the story they're selling.

Quality of Earnings Report

We analyze historical financials, normalize one-time items, validate EBITDA add-backs, and identify sustainable vs. non-recurring earnings.

Working Capital Analysis

We establish normalized working capital, identify seasonal patterns, and help you structure the WC peg to avoid post-close disputes.

Risk Identification

We flag concentration risks, margin pressures, accounting irregularities, and operational issues that could impact post-close performance.

Deal Support

We help you translate findings into negotiating points, price adjustments, and protective deal terms.
