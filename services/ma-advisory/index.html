Our M&A Process

A disciplined approach that protects your downside while capturing opportunity.

Strategy & Targeting

We define your acquisition criteria, build target lists, and develop outreach campaigns. For sellers, we position the business and identify qualified buyers.

Analysis & Valuation

We build detailed financial models, assess quality of earnings, identify red flags, and develop defensible valuations that support your negotiating position.

Structuring & Negotiation

From LOI through definitive docs, we help you structure deals that protect your interests—earnouts, escrows, working capital mechanisms, reps and warranties.

Close & Integration

We coordinate the closing process, manage funds flow, and help you plan Day 1 integration so you hit the ground running.
